What is GenkiWars?

GenkiWars is a NFT Cardano metaverse where every NFT is unique and have a utility. GenkiWars will have its own minting, staking platform made by its own software engineers.

How does GenkiWars staking work?

Genkis, Genkivices and Genkieggs are stakable NFTs to earn $GENKI points. Rewards are calculated by a Genki algorithm with POWR attributes multipled by luck to receive $GENKI points per 15 days.

$GENKI points are Cardano native tokens, you can use the Genki points to buy Genkieggs, Genkis and even rare Genki PFPs.
